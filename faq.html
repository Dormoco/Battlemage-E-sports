Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEMS International?

GEMS International is an organization dedicated to empowering young people aged 12-20 through education, skill development, and extracurricular activities, including e-sports.

What does GEMS International focus on?

It focuses on academic growth, leadership skills, talent development, and competitive opportunities for young individuals.

Is GEMS International only about e-sports?

No, GEMS International supports various activities, including academic programs, skill-building workshops, leadership training, and e-sports competitions.

What is the mission of GEMS International?

To empower young individuals by providing them with opportunities to develop skills, explore their potential, and compete at high levels in various fields.

How does GEMS International support young people?

By providing training, mentorship, competitions, and community support to help youth excel in their chosen fields.

Who can join GEMS International?

Anyone between the ages of 12-20 who is passionate about learning, growing, and competing in different activities.

Does GEMS International offer scholarships?

Yes, GEMS provides scholarships and sponsorships for talented individuals in e-sports and other fields.

Is GEMS International a nonprofit organization?

Yes, it operates as a nonprofit, investing in youth development and providing opportunities at no cost to eligible participants.

How can I join GEMS International?

You can apply through the official website, participate in community events, or contact their team via email.

Where can I find more information about GEMS International?

You can check out their website, Discord community, and official announcements.
